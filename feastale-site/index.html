Meet Palaharappetti

Palaharappetti is Kerala’s authentic snack gift pack, crafted with love and tradition, making it the perfect choice for corporate gifting. Each pack brings together the irresistible taste of Banana Chips, Sharkaravaratti, and Baby Murukku—beautifully packed and customizable with your company logo and greeting card. Whether it’s for festivals, employee appreciation, client relationships, or corporate events, Palaharappetti adds a touch of Kerala’s warmth and flavor to every occasion.

Our Story

Feastale originated in the minds of three young entrepreneurs under LEAD Business Incubator. Palaharappetti is our first step — a playful love letter to Kerala.
